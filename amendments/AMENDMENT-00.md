> This amendment is hereby ratified as the foundational and binding process governing all constitutional changes.
>
> Its authority is declarative, normative, and precedential.

## Scope

Any modification to this constitution, including its principles or articles, must be recorded as an amendment.

## Governance Rule

Amendments are additive, historically preserved, and never overwrite prior constitutional text.

Each amendment must explicitly state its purpose, scope, affected sections, and rationale.

## Accountability

No amendment is valid without explicit human authorship, deliberate approval, and a traceable commit record.
